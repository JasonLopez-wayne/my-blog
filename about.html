Hey, I'm Jason!

Let's cut to the chase-you love music or clicked on a wrong link. Anyway, welcome! I'm the man behind these reviews, and yeah, I spend way too much time obsessing over albums. But who needs sleep when you have the sweet, sweet sounds of experimental R&B to keep you going?

Fun fact: I once spent a whole week listening to one album, just to figure out if that one weird sound was a trumpet or some strange, alien instrument. Yeah, spoiler alert-still have no clue. When not chasing those notes, catch me at a concert or drowned in coffee, desperately trying to write a review before I listen to *just one more song*-it's never just one.

But in reality, it's a labor of love-a small nook of the internet whereby I get to be a music nerd and share what's on my brain with other music nerds, such as yourself. Yep, you! Take some time now and read some reviews, or say hi if you'd like to chat with me about your latest and greatest song obsessions-or share theories on what the heck that weird sound is.
