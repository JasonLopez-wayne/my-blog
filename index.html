Orquídeas

Artist: Kali Uchis

Release Date: January 12th, 2024

Review: If music were a flower, this album would be a tropical orchid: rare and beautiful, with just that little something extra. Kali Uchis doesn't hold anything back in 'Orquídeas', lusciously full of dreamy vocals and hypnotic beats. It's perfect for that moment when, dramatically, you stare out the window at a thunderstorm outside, pretending it's an indie film and you're the star.

The album is like a sweet daydream that lingers even after the world snaps back into focus. Recommended listening: in the shower, with extra steam for maximum dramatic effect.

Hit me Hard and Soft

Artist: Billie Eilish

Release Date: May 17th, 2024

Review: Billie comes at you from two directions in this one, as the title suggests. ‘Hit Me Hard and Soft’ is an emotional rollercoaster that somehow manages to be both a gentle whisper and a smack in the face — and we love it for that. Picture yourself staring at the ceiling in the middle of the night, pondering life’s mysteries while the beats echo in your brain.

It’s perfect for those late-night musings when you’re feeling introspective, yet slightly dramatic. Crank it up, lay back, and let Billie remind you why feeling *all* the feels is perfectly normal.
